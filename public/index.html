Michael Abdelshahid

The Life of Being a Hard-of-Hearing Egyptian-American

The timeline of Michael Abdelshahid's life.

  • 1989 - Michael Abdelshahid, known as Nader, was born on September 5 in Cleveland, Ohio.
  • 1992 - Michael was profounded as hard-of-hearing by the doctor at the Cleveland Clinic Metro Hospital.
  • 1993 - Michael went to school as a preschooler at A.G.Bell Elementary School. From there, he used hearing-aids with FM radio to hear the teachers use the microphone.
  • 1997 - Michael's deaf program transferred from A. G. Bell Elementary School to William Rainey Harper School from 1st grade until the end of 3rd grade.
  • 2000 - At age 10, Michael's deaf program traferred back to A. G. Bell Elementary School.
  • 2008 - Michael graduated from High School and attended Rochester Institute of Technology for his undergrad.
  • 2014 - Michael graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with an Associate's degree in Labratory Science Technology and Bachelor's degree in Biology.
